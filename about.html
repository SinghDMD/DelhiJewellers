Who we are

We make great things happen.

At Delhi Jewellers, every piece of jewellery tells a story – of traditions, of moments, of milestones. We invite you to be a part of our story and let us become a part of yours.

Tradition Meets Trust in Point Cook, Melbourne

See what set us apart.

Nestled in the heart of Point Cook, Melbourne, Delhi Jewellers stands as a beacon of trust, quality, and authenticity. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the timeless elegance of Indian-styled gold jewellery to the vibrant community of Victoria, and over the years, we've become a cherished destination for those seeking pieces that resonate with cultural richness and craftsmanship.

Our founder, Navpreet Kaur, envisioned a place where beauty, elegance, and tradition converge. Inspired by the intricate designs and rich heritage of Punjab, every piece at Delhi Jewellers is a testament to the artistry of Indian jewellery-making. But beyond the allure of gold and gemstones, we've built our reputation on a foundation of trust and integrity.

Our process is transparent

As a registered second-hand dealer and pawnbroker (Registration No. SHD-0017435), we operate with the utmost transparency and adherence to state and federal laws. Our commitment to ethical practices extends to every facet of our business.

We listen to our clients carefully

What truly sets us apart is our deep-rooted belief in inclusivity. We respect and welcome individuals from all walks of life, ensuring that every interaction is conducted with dignity, respect, and professionalism. Our team is here to guide and assist you.

We provide custom solutions

From the moment you engage with us, be it a simple inquiry or an intricate custom design request, we're committed to making your experience memorable, meaningful, and above all, inclusive.

Our Mission

Navpreet, with her unwavering passion for both the craft and the community, leads a team that embodies these values. We are committed to providing exceptional service and value to every person who walks through our doors.
