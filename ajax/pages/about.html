Tradition Meets Trust in Altona, Melbourne

See what set us apart.

Nestled in the heart of Altona, Melbourne, Delhi Jewellers stands as a beacon of trust, quality, and authenticity. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the timeless elegance of Indian-styled gold jewellery to the vibrant community of Victoria, and over the years, we've become a cherished destination for those seeking pieces that resonate with cultural richness and craftsmanship.

Our founder, Navpreet Kaur, envisioned a place where beauty, elegance, and tradition converge. Inspired by the intricate designs and rich heritage of Punjab, every piece at Delhi Jewellers is a testament to the artistry of Indian jewellery-making. But beyond the allure of gold and gemstones, we've built our reputation on a foundation of trust and integrity.
