Delhi Jewellers Privacy Policy
Effective Date: Oct-2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Delhi Jewellers. Your privacy is paramount to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal data. This privacy policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

When you visit our website, we may gather certain information, including:

Technical data such as browser type and version.
Usage data detailing how you interact with our website.
3. How We Use Your Data

We employ the data we collect for several reasons:

To ensure the proper functioning and security of our website.
To inform you about updates to our website or services.
To enhance your user experience.
To analyze website usage for internal review.
For remarketing purposes, allowing us to provide targeted advertisements.
4. Google reCAPTCHA

Our website incorporates Google reCAPTCHA to verify that interactions on our site are made by genuine users and not automated systems. Google reCAPTCHA may gather specific data to make this distinction. For more details on Google reCAPTCHA, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

We utilize cookies and similar tracking tools to monitor activity on our website. Cookies are small data files that can be used as unique identifiers. If you prefer, you can set your browser to decline cookies, but this may affect certain features of our website.

6. Data Sharing

We uphold a strict policy of not sharing your personal data with third parties, except when necessary for our services’ functionality or as required by law. We never sell your personal data.

7. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and implement various measures to prevent unauthorized access or use.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

From time to time, we may update our privacy policy. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Any modifications made will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.
